Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026. This policy explains what information is collected through this website and how it is used.

Who this covers

This website is operated by FORMA Studio ("FORMA", "we", "us"). This policy applies to visitors to this website and to anyone who submits information through the contact form.

Information collected through the contact form

When you submit the contact form, the following information is collected: name, business name, email address, current website URL (if provided), a description of your business, what you would like help with, estimated budget and preferred launch timeframe.

This information is used only to respond to your enquiry, discuss a potential project and, if you choose to proceed, deliver that project. It is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form processing

Contact form submissions are sent using a third-party form-handling service (for example Formspree or Netlify Forms, depending on how this site is deployed). That service processes the submitted data in order to deliver it by email and is bound by its own privacy and security practices.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not set tracking cookies and does not use third-party analytics or advertising scripts as delivered. If analytics or cookie-based tools are added in future, this policy will be updated to reflect that.

Data retention

Enquiry details are kept only for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry and, where a project goes ahead, for the duration of that working relationship and any legal or accounting requirements afterwards.

Your rights

You can ask what information is held about you, request a correction, or ask for it to be deleted at any time by emailing the address below.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@formastudio.co.uk.
